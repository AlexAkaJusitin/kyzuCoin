HOW TO PICK A WALLET

If you’re planning to buy, trade or invest in cryptocurrencies, you’ll need crypto wallets to store them. While they serve similar functions, a crypto wallet differs from a regular wallet. So, what is a crypto wallet? It’s a physical or digital storage device for the proof of the cryptocurrency you own. Unlike a regular wallet, it doesn’t store physical coins and bills. Rather, it holds the public and private keys required to authenticate and transfer your cryptocurrencies.
